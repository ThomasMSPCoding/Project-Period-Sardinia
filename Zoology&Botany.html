Zoology & Botany

Sardinia is home to many different species of animals and plants. It is truly a birdwatcher’s paradise and nature enjoyer’s heaven. During your visit, make sure to meet some of the most wonderful animals on the island:

The beautiful colonies of Greater Flamingos (Phoenicopterus roseus) are a must-see while you are visiting Sardinia. They can be observed in the Molentargius-Saline Park and other wetland coastal areas around the island. Be sure you do not miss them!

Figure 1: Sardinianbeaches, 2023

A species of lizards that inhabit only Corsica and Sardinia can also be seen during your visit! Fitzinger's Algyroides (Algyroides fitzingeri) are a small lizard species that can be found in Mediterranean shrubbery, temperate forests and shrubland. Not a whole lot of them are sighted and described so maybe you can help! (iNaturalist NZ, n.d.)
