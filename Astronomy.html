Astronomy

For all astronomy fans and fanatics Sardinia should definitely make the shortlist of locations to visit for its notable stargazing skies. Due to significantly low light pollution, Sardinia has one of the darkest skies in Europe. This is much to the advantage of the various planetariums, observatories and telescope centers found around the island. In fact the largest European radio telescope is located near San Basilio in southern Sardinia. Not only do these locations offer tours and educational excursions suitable for various demographics and academic expertise but are also actively used in the scientific research field to investigate and record weather, sun environment and the likes (MULAS, 2023)

Alongside the below mentioned locations, there are a plethora of desolate locations where light pollution is practically zero. A few examples include: Teulada area, inland of Gallura, the Ogliastra and Bosa (Skies of Sardinia, 2021)

Planetario dell’Unione Sarda

This is an indoor planetarium offering educational events about the solar system, its planets and celestial bodies. They are continually updating the set ups as new discoveries or problems arise within the astronomy field. They aim to inform and provide sufficient knowledge, shortening the distance between complex themes or concepts and their misconceptions. They also run courses for both adults and children of all ages and educational backgrounds (Il Planetario, 2025).

Address: Pza L'Unione Sarda, 09122 Cagliari CA, Italy

For more information and ticket sales: Planetario dell’Unione Sarda

Source:L’Unione Sarda.It, 2022

Osservatorio Astronomico - Monte Armidda

This sky observatorium boasts one of the darkest skies of Europe. Built in 1986 it is run by volunteers who share a passion for astronomy with their visitors. There is also a planetarium that most people frequent in the colder winter months. Visits are recommended to be scheduled in advance particularly off season. During the high season when tourism is flourishing many major cultural events are organised.

Address: Loc. Monte Armidda, 08040 Gairo Sant'Elena NU, Italy

For more information and bookings: Osservatorio Astronomico - Monte Armidda

Source:Luna Rossa, You Tube 2018

Sardinia Radio Telescope

This is a 64- meter dish radio telescope. It is capable of carrying out radio astronomy, geodynamical studies and space science. It was initially used solely for technical and scientific purposes but now also as a place of education and observation. They also organise events and festivals all-things stargazing and astronomy which are updated and posted on their facebook site. These are family friendly events, educational for all ages and interests. On their website they also have live weather updates and a live webcam for real-time information.

Address: Casa Cantoniera di Pranu Sanguni, Località, 09040 San Basilio CA, Italy

For more information visit: Facebook: Sardinia Radio Telescope

Source:L’Unione Sarda, 2024

Osservatorio Astronomico di Cagliari

Another beautiful observatory which is one of the 17 facilities of the research for the National Institute of Astrophysics. There is also a library inside which hosts some 10,000 paper books mostly concerned with Astronomical and Astrophysical fields. It is a location of extensive research and whose researchers run events concerning astrophysics for both tourists and professionals in the field. On their website they have updates of events planned in the coming year so for more information make sure to visit it.

Address: INAF-Osservatorio Astronomico di Cagliari, Via della Scienza 5 - I-09047 Selargius (Cagliari)

For more info visit: Osservatorio Astronomico di Cagliari

Source:L’Unione Sarda, 2024

Carolina Ranch Escursioni e Tour

Slightly different from the other observatories and more oriented towards more touristic and family adventures. This seaside location offers tours on horseback or quad bikes and organises skygazing evenings with telescopes as well as archaeological excursions to the must see sites in Sardinia. It is an exciting and multi-disciplinary approach to a holiday with a mix of relaxation and discovery. Beginners fear not! The ranch offers introductory horseback riding to accommodate all levels and expertise.

In advance booking is recommended before the holiday!

Address: Locality Cala Sapone 11 - 09017 Sant'Antioco Sardinia

For more information visit: Carolina Ranch Escursioni e Tour

Source:Tripadvisor, 2023

Source:Carolina Ranch, 2023
